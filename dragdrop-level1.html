ABOUT THE GAME

Race against the clock to match products with the correct icons on each level. Make a mistake, and you'll lose valuable seconds.

Are you up for the challenge?

HOW TO PLAY:

• Tap or drag the products into the correct icon.
• Drag product to an incorrect icon – (X) amount of time will be deducted.
• Get them all correct to level up.

NB: There are 3 exciting levels to conquer in this game.
